የእግዚአብሔር ብርሃን ቤተክርስትያን (The Light of God Church) is an Ethiopian and Eritrean Evangelical Church located in Karlsruhe, Germany.

We worship together every Sunday in Amharic, Tigrinya, and German and welcome all believers seeking God’s presence, fellowship, and spiritual growth.

Our Mission

  • To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ
  • To support Ethiopian and Eritrean Christian families
  • To build unity, love, and spiritual growth in our community
