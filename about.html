Home About Contact

Licht Gottes Gemeinde (የእግዚአብሔር ብርሃን ቤተክርስትያን) is an Ethiopian and Eritrean Evangelical Church located in Karlsruhe, Germany. Our community was founded to share the light of God with believers from Ethiopia, Eritrea, and beyond who live in Karlsruhe and the surrounding area.

We worship together every Sunday in Amharic, Tigrinya, and German, and we welcome everyone who seeks fellowship, prayer, and the Word of God. Our mission is to bring people closer to Jesus Christ through love, unity, and service.

Whether you are new to Karlsruhe or have been living here for years, you are warmly invited to join us for worship and community.

Our Mission

  • To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • To build a spiritual home for Ethiopian and Eritrean believers.
  • To serve our community through love and faith.
