Licht Gottes Gemeinde (የእግዚአብሔር ብርሃን ቤተክርስትያን) is an Ethiopian and Eritrean Evangelical Church in Karlsruhe, Germany.

We worship together every Sunday in Amharic, Tigrinya, and German and welcome everyone seeking God's presence.

Our Mission

  • To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ
  • To support Ethiopian/Eritrean Christian families
  • To build unity, love, and spiritual growth
