About Us

የእግዚአብሔር ብርሃን ቤተክርስትያን (The Light of God Church) is an Ethiopian and Eritrean Evangelical Church serving the community of Karlsruhe, Germany. Our mission is to share the light of God through worship, prayer, and service. We welcome all believers who speak Amharic, Tigrinya, or German to join our services and Bible study.
